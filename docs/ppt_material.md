## Slide 2: The Challenge
*   **Safety is Hard**: 2.3 million industrial accidents occur annually.
*   **Monitoring is Broken**: Human monitoring is expensive, error-prone, and reactive.
*   **Goal**: Zero Accidents through Proactive AI.

## Slide 3: Our Solution
*   **Real-Time AI**: Detects "Missing Helmet" and "Missing Vest" in milliseconds.
*   **Automated Logging**: 100% digital audit trail.
*   **Granular Tracking**: Tracks individual workers (Person ID) to identify repeat offenders.

## Slide 4: Live Demo (Key Features)
*   **Multi-Camera Switching**: Seamlessly manage multiple sites.
*   **The "Red Box"**: Instant visual feedback on violations.
*   **The Logbook**: Searchable history of all safety incidents.

## Slide 5: Tech Stack
*   **Vision**: YOLOv8 + ByteTrack (State-of-the-Art accuracy).
*   **Backend**: FastAPI (High performance Python).
*   **Frontend**: React (Modern, responsive UI).
*   **Infrastructure**: Docker + NVIDIA CUDA.

## Slide 6: Future Roadmap
1.  **Face Recognition**: Identify *who* the worker is (requires consent).
2.  **Zone Logic**: "Authorized Personnel Only" zones.
3.  **Floor Map**: Real-time 2D map of worker positions.
4.  **SMS Alerts**: Send text messages to site supervisors.
